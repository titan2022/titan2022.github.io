"Titan Robotics, FRC Team 2022, inspires leadership and technical capability in members through rigorous FIRST competition training, outreach demonstrations, and volunteering with local youth in STEM."

FRC Team 2022, Titan Robotics, is a high school robotics team made up of students from the Illinois Mathematics and Science Academy (IMSA). Located in Aurora, IL, IMSA is a residential high school that accepts gifted students from across the state, making our backgrounds diverse and impacts far-reaching. We compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) and other off-season competitions, which teaches our members technical skills, such as mechanical, programming, electrical, and Computer Aided Design (CAD). Team members also have the opportunity to develop business, leadership, and communication skills.
