Located in Aurora, IL, IMSA is a residential high school that accepts gifted students from across the state, making our backgrounds diverse and impacts far-reaching. We compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) and other off-season competitions, which teaches our members technical skills, such as mechanical, programming, electrical, and computer aided design (CAD), as well as soft skills involving business, leadership, and communication.
